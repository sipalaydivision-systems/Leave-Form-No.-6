Privacy Policy

SDO Sipalay City Leave Management System

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

The Schools Division Office (SDO) Sipalay City is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the SDO Sipalay City Leave Management System, we collect and process the following information:

  • Personal Information: Full name, employee ID, position, school/office assignment
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, home address
  • Employment Information: Date of hire, salary grade, employment status
  • Leave Records: Leave applications, leave balances, leave history
  • Digital Signatures: Electronic signatures for form authentication
  • Login Credentials: Username and encrypted password

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and managing leave applications
  • Maintaining accurate leave credit records
  • Generating official leave documents (Civil Service Form No. 6)
  • Communicating about your leave application status
  • Complying with Civil Service Commission (CSC) requirements
  • Administrative and statistical reporting
  • System security and user authentication

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Your consent when creating an account
  • Compliance with legal obligations under Civil Service rules and regulations
  • Legitimate interests of the Department of Education for personnel management
  • Performance of our public function as a government agency

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information may be shared with:

  • Authorized Personnel: HR officers, supervisors, and approving authorities within SDO Sipalay City
  • DepEd Offices: Regional and Central Office as required for personnel matters
  • Government Agencies: Civil Service Commission, GSIS, and other agencies as required by law
  • Auditing Bodies: Commission on Audit for official audits

Note: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties for commercial purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular system security updates
  • Activity logging and monitoring

6. Data Retention

Your personal information and leave records are retained in accordance with government records retention policies. Employment and leave records are typically maintained for the duration of your employment and for a period thereafter as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Complaint: File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission

8. Cookies and Tracking

This system uses session cookies to maintain your login state and ensure proper functionality. These cookies are essential for the operation of the system and do not track your activity outside of this application.

9. Children's Privacy

This system is intended for use by DepEd employees only and is not designed for use by children under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact:

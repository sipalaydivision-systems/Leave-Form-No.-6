SDO Sipalay City Leave Management System

Overview

The Schools Division Office (SDO) Sipalay City Leave Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline and automate the leave application process for all employees under the Department of Education - SDO Sipalay City. This system replaces the traditional paper-based leave application process with an efficient, transparent, and user-friendly online solution.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, efficient, and transparent leave management experience for all DepEd employees in SDO Sipalay City, ensuring proper documentation, timely processing, and accurate leave credit tracking.

Key Features

  • Online Leave Application - Submit Civil Service Form No. 6 (Application for Leave) electronically
  • Real-time Leave Credit Tracking - Monitor your vacation leave (VL) and sick leave (SL) balances
  • Multi-level Approval Workflow - Automatic routing through HR, ASDS, and SDS for approval
  • Digital Leave Cards - Access and view your complete leave history anytime
  • Automated Notifications - Receive updates on your application status
  • Printable Forms - Generate properly formatted leave forms for official records

System Users

  • Employees - Teaching and non-teaching personnel who apply for leaves
  • Human Resources (HR) - Reviews applications and manages leave credits
  • Administrative Officer (AO) - Processes certifications and records
  • Assistant Schools Division Superintendent (ASDS) - Provides recommendations
  • Schools Division Superintendent (SDS) - Final approval authority
  • IT Administrator - System maintenance and user management

About SDO Sipalay City

The Schools Division Office of Sipalay City is a field office of the Department of Education (DepEd) responsible for the delivery of basic education services in Sipalay City, Negros Occidental. We are committed to providing quality, accessible, relevant, and liberating basic education for all.
